FAQs

How much experience do I need?

None, just bring a willingness to learn!

How do I become a member?

Members should fill out the membership form at the bottom of the page. No membership fees are required for you to join.

Why should I join this club?

To engage in lively conversations regarding different investment opportunities and expressing your passion for the business world.

Does the club make actual investments?

No, as a club we do not make financial investments. Our goal is to encourage discourse about investing.
